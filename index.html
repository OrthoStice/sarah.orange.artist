About

Sarah Orange is a Hopkins County native, and a graduate of Madisonville North Hopkins. She earned her BA in Studio Art from the University of Louisville, Magna Cum Laude, in 2004. She is a member of the Hopkins County Art League and has been integral in several of their successful events, such as the Student Art Show in the spring of this year and the Madisonville gallery hop?. Her work in the form of murals, sculpture, and paintings has been frequently commissioned. You can find more of her work at Sarah.Orange.Artist on Facebook.
